Who are Tots?

Tots are creatures that look like us but with a big difference - each of them has powers over one of eight natural domains. They can use magic spells or physical attacks related to their element.

What is Aptos Lenegends?

Aptos Legends is a role-playing game. It relies on the new Aptos blockchain. The game will be playable in app form on PC, Mac, Android and eventually iOS. The visual style of Aptos Legends was inspired by universally acclaimed classics - Stardew Valley, Pokemon, Zelda and more.
